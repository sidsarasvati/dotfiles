## KNOWN LIMITATIONS

### 1. Core Stability Bottleneck (PRIMARY)
**The Issue**: Low back compensates when core fatigues
**Detection**: "Low back takes all pain" during squats/RDLs
**Impact**: Limits leg progression, causes injury risk
**Fix**: Daily Kaizen core work + "Core tight" cue before every rep

### 2. Glute Medius Weakness
**The Issue**: Causes knee collapse + lower back compensation
**Detection**: Knee drifting inward during squats, lower back feeling before workout
**Impact**: Anterior pelvic tilt pattern, injury risk
**Fix**: Single-leg RDL activation FIRST in every warmup

### 3. Anterior Pelvic Tilt Pattern
**The Issue**: Weak glutes → pelvis tips forward → lower back overloaded
**Detection**: Lower back feeling before workout = glutes not firing
**Impact**: Limits all standing exercises
**Fix**: Standing glute squeezes (20 reps) at start of every warmup

### 4. ARM STRETCH Injury Pattern (Current - Healing)
**Origin**: Nov 3, 2025 (Session 023) - Chest strain
**Current Status**: 30+ days healing, improving but not 100%
**What's OK**: Push movements (pushing away from body)
**What Triggers**: ARM STRETCH positions:
  - DB press bottom position (arms extended)
  - RDL hang position (arms with weight)
  - Overhead straight arm movements
  - DB rows (arm hangs with 50lb = original injury mechanism)
**Adaptation**: Skip lateral raises, modify RDL form (hands forward), use inverted rows instead of DB rows

### 5. Bicep Stance Issue (SOLVED)
**The Issue**: Low back activation during bicep curls
**Root Cause**: Stance/posture, not the exercise
**Fix**: Wide stance principle (Session 020 discovery)

### 6. Face Pull Form Issues
**The Issue**: Neck strain when form breaks
**Root Cause**: Core disengagement causes compensation
**Fix**: 25lbs good form > 35lbs bad form
